My name is Tom Hickey and my focus is on improving patient engagement through technology.


I am a team lead manager at Epic Systems for the MyChart application. Through my experience with process optimization via my internships at Amazon.com and my problem solving skills developed while earning a degree in mechanical engineering, I drive our customers to achieve outcomes through effective use of the system.

While not at work I play rugby and basketball, read biographies and science fiction, and watch too many movies featuring Jean Claude Van Damme, Arnold Schwarzenegger, and the like.
